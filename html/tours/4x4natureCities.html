VILLES IMPÉRIALES & NATURE TOUR

(By 4x4 or minibus)

Overview

Difficulty
Duration
9 days
Best season
All year round except when heavy snowfall
Accomodation
Hotel/Riad/Auberge
Places
Maximum 4 pax/4x4 or 8 pax/minibus
Price
On demand

Day 1: Fez

Arrival at the Saiss Airport. Transfer to the hotel or riad

Day 2: Fez

Day off to explore the city: medina, tanneries, souks, medersa, …. Overnight stay in a hotel or riad.

Day 3: Fez – Chefchaouen (200km)

Departure after breakfast to the Blue City, Chefchaouen, hidden in the north of Morocco. The highlight of this city is the old medina straight from a fairytal, where almost everything is painted blue. Overnight stay in a riad in the medina.

Day 4: Chefchaouen – Meknes (200km)

After breakfast we will take the road to Meknes. On the way there we visit the beautiful archeological site of Volubilis and the city of Moulay Idriss, where we climb up the alleys to admire the view of the wide landscape and to see what the mosque looks like from above. Transfer to Meknes, free visit of the city. Overnight stay in a hotel/riad.

Day 5: Meknes – Oulmès – Tarmilate (170km)

We take the road to Khemisset where we turn towards Oulmès (mineral source). Possibility to drive to the Ras el Ma source. Overnight stay in the hotel in Tarmilate.

Day 6: Tarmilate – Bin el Ouidane (250km)

Passing via Khenifra, Kasba-Tadla on our way to the large and beautiful lake of the Barrage Bin el Ouidane. Possibility to take a lakeside walk. Overnight in an auberge on the lakeside.

Day 7: Bin el Ouidane – Marrakech (230km)

On the way to Marrakech we will visit the Ouzoud waterfalls. Overnight stay in a riad in the medina.

Day 8: Marrakech

Our last day is a day off to visit the Red City of Marrakech: Medersa Ben Youssef, the Tombs of the Saadians, the Bahia Palace, the el Badi Palace, the Museum of Marrakech, the Majorelle Gardens,… Overnight stay in a riad in the medina

Day 9: Marrakech

Got to catch that airplane!
