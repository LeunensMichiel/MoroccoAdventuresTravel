LAKES OF THE ATLAS

(By 4x4 or minibus)

Overview

Difficulty
Duration
10 days
Best season
May to October
Accommodation
Hotel/Riad/Auberge
Places
Maximum 4 pax/4x4 or 8 pax/minibus
Price
On demand

Day 1: Marrakech

Arrival at the Menara airport. Transfer to the hotel or riad.

Day 2: Marrakech – Aguelmame Ouiouane (1650m) (366km)

Departure after breakfast by 4WD or minibus via Beni Mellal, Khénifra to the Ouiouane Lake in the Middle Atlas. Overnight stay in local gîte.

Day 3: Aguelmame Ouiouane

Free day for walking and exploring the beautiful area around the lake. Overnight stay in a local gîte.

Day 4: Aguelmame Ouiouane – Imouzzer-du-kandar (130km)

After breakfast we’ll take the road passing by the Ifran NP, Cedar forests, the ski resort of Mischliffen and the Valley of Rocks to Imouzzer-du-Kandar. Overnight stay in an hotel or appartment.

Day 5: Imouzzer-du-kandar

Today we’ll drive to the Ifrah Lake for a walk. In the area there are more “Dayets” (Lakes) such as the Aoua Lake and the Hachlaf Lake which we will visit according to the time that remains. Overnight stay in Imouzzer-du-kandar.

Day 6: Imilchil

We will pack a picnic and go for a daywalk around the 2 beautiful lakes Isli and Tislit (a Berber Romeo & Julia-story). Overnight stay in the auberge.

Day 7: Imilchil – Bin el Ouidane (130km)

Today we will take the road passing the Col Tizi-n’Isly and drive to the South (visiting on our way the Rock Cathedral, aso called Mastfrane Cathedral) to the large and beautiful lake of the Barrage Bin el Ouidane. Overnight stay in an auberge at the lake.

Day 8: Bin el Ouidane – Ouzoud Falls - Marrakech (230km)

On the way to Marrakech we will visit the Ouzoud waterfalls. Overnight stay in a riad in the medina.

Day 9: Marrakech

Got to catch that airplane!
