AROUND THE LAKES

Overview

Difficulty
Duration
7 or 8 days
Best season
May to October
Accomodation
Hotel/Riad, gîte
Price
On demand

Day 1: Marrakech

Arrival at the Menara airport. Transfer to the hotel or Riad

Day 2: Marrakech – Aguelmame Ouiouane (1650m) (366km)

Departure after breakfast by 4WD or minibus via Beni Mellal, Khénifra to the Ouiouane Lake in the Middle Atlas. Overnight stay in local gîte.

Day 3: Aguelmame Ouiouane – Zaouit Ifran Falls (4hours walk)

We cross the forests to descend to the waterfall Zaouit Ifran for a picnic. Zaouia d'Ifrane is a Berber village, known for its cedar and oak forests, waterfalls, beautiful plateaus and cliffs. Return late afternoon by car, overnight stay in gîte.

Day 4: Aguelmame Ouiouane – Sources Oum er Rbia (4hours walk)

We will continue our trek through the cedar forest on a mule track. This forest sheltered the lions of the Atlas, as well as tigers, which disappeared around the 70’s. We will follow a valley that leads us to the gorges from which spring the 40 sources of Oum er Rbia; a magical place where caves have been set up along the sources. Picnic and return late afternoon by car. Overnight stay in gîte.

Day 5: Aguelmame Ouiouane - Aguelmame Tiguelmamine (1650m) – Aguelmame Azigza (1487m) (4hours walk)

DWe are still in the Zayan Country, a famous tribe in the history of Morocco, very proud and very welcoming population. We follow a path that crosses centennial cedar forests. These forests are home to a few dozen pairs of monkeys, which we approach easily. Many sheepfolds near the pastures, superb view on the mountain and the cedar forest near the Ighoud-sources. Picnic and return late afternoon by car.

Day 5 or 6: Tighremt n’Aït Ahmed – Imi Nirkt (2010m)

Beautiful day of walking in the M’goun Valley through many villages, campsite in Imi Nirkt at the entrance of the M’goun Canyon.

Day 6 or 7: Imi Nirkt – Tighranimin (1770m)

Walking in the river through one of the most spectacular areas of southern Morocco, the M’goun Canyon.

Day 6: Aguelmame Ouiouane – Fez (127 km)

Departure after breakfast via Azrou and Ifrane to Fez. Day off to explore the city: medina, tanneries, souks, medersa,…. Overnight stay in a hotel or riad.

Day 7: Fez – Marrakech (515km)

When returning from Fez, the journey ends in Fez. If you return from Marrakech, we will leave after breakfast for a long drive to Marrakech. Overnight stay in a hotel or riad.

Day 8: Marrakech – return flight

Got to catch that airplane!
