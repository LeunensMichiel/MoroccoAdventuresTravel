CROSSING SAGHRO MOUNTAINS

Overview

Difficulty
Duration
6/7 days
Best season
October to April
Accomodation
Hotel/Riad, gîte, tents
Price
On demand

On this hike you will explore the Jebel Saghro. During a 4-day trip you’ll cross the Tagdilt Plateau, climb to the summit of Jebel Kouaouch and walk along Bab n’Ali, Irhazzoun and the Taoudacht Valley to Nkob. (This trek can be extended by a few days.)

This trek has two options available.

Option 1

Day 0: Marrakech – Tagdilt (1660m) (330 km)

After breakfast, transfer by minibus or 4WD via the Tizi n’Tichkapass, Ouarzazate and Boumalne Dadès (route of 1000 Kasbahs) to Tagdilt, the starting point of our hike where we will meet the muleteers. Overnight stay in a local gîte.

Day 1: Tagdilt – Isk n’Iferd (2400m)

On our first hiking day we will gradually walk up from the plain around Tagdilt to our first bivouac in the mountains.

Option 2

Day 1: Ouarzazate – Tagdilt – Isk n’Iferd (2400m)

Early morning transfer by minibus or 4WD from Ouarzazate, via Boumalne Dadès (route of 1000 Kasbahs) to Tagdilt, the starting point of our hike where we will meet the muleteers. After lunch we will gradually walk up from the plain around Tagdilt to our first bivouac in the mountains.

Day 2: Isk n’Iferd – Igli (1730m)

Steady climb up to cross the Jebel Kouaouch (2592m). Looking out over the mountains, we will see the snowy peaks of the High Atlas in the north and the beginning of the desert in the southeast. We’ll start descending to Igli. Overnight stay in gîte or tents.

Day 3: Igli - Irhazzoun (1275m)

Today we’ll walk up and down to the large rock called Bab n’Ali (“gate of Ali”) at 1550m, from which we have a breath-taking view of the Sahgro. We will continue to the Tadaout n’Tablah massif with his jagged eroded rocks, before slowly descending to the village of Irhazzoun. Overnight stay in tents.

Day 4: Irhazzoun – Nkob

On our last hiking day we will gradually leave the mountains but we can still fully enjoy the views to the village of Handour (1098m). After Handour we’ll descend further to Nkob. Overnight stay in a beautiful auberge.

Day 5: Nkob – Marrakech or Ouarzazate

Transfer by 4WD or minibus to Marrakech or Ouarzazate, last night in a hotel or riad.

Day 6: Marrakech or Ouarzazate

Got to catch that airplane!
