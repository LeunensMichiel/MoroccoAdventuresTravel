Ourika Valley

The Ourika Valley is about 60km by road from Marrakech in Morrocco. Situated in the foothills of the High Atlas, it contains a number of Berber villages, female co-operatives manufacturing Argan oil and a set of waterfalls near the village of Ourika itself. Most people will head up the valley to visit the waterfalls at Setti Fatma (+/- 2h).
