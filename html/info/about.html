Who are we?

Abdo and Martine @ your service

Based in Ouarzazate, we offer original, tailor-made treks and tours whereby we take your wishes into account as much as possible. Please find some examples of treks and tours on this page.

Our goal is to connect people, to get to know and understand the Moroccon Berber culture and hospitality, and this in an amazing, beautiful landscape with respect for nature & environment, local people and local economy. We are never far from the real Berber life, we gladly accept the invitations from the locals and nomads to drink tea together, we encourage children to play games together, we visit local schools, local crafts, local souks,... We hope that after a great journey, you’ll return home with your own painting of impressions and memories.

“Au Maroc nous n'avons pas l'heure, nous avons le temps”

Born in a small village between the Atlas and the Saghro-massif, Abdo knows the mountains by heart.

He is specialized in hiking since more than 15 years, leads you through small donkey-trails and wherever possible avoids the bigger slopes. He is looking for the best campsites and his accompanying cook prepares the most delicious fresh meals. Authenticity and great personal support are guaranteed by Abdo and his team of mule-drivers to provide you a unique experience in a wondrous region, where you can taste the Berber culture, the natives’ hospitality and the stunning scenery. Walking is up to you, everything else is taken care of by Abdo and his team! Pure enjoyment!

Abdo is also an experienced driver with a tourism permit. He will bring you by 4WD or minibus through the most impressive landscapes of the High, Middle & Anti-Atlas and the desert to the most beautiful cities and villages of Morocco. Abdo speaks Tachelhit (Berber), Arab, French and Spanish fluently and tries to master Dutch and English.

“Quote van Martine"

Martine, Belgian by birth, Berber through her marriage to Abdo. Because of the many hikes in Morocco she fell in love with the country and by extension with the local Berber population.

She is fascinated by the beautiful nature, the Berber hospitality and the rich culture of Morocco and definitely wants to share these experiences with other travelers.

Martine is the right hand of Abdo for the bookings and planning, for setting up the desired circuits and is always looking for new, original and authentic destinations and highlights all around the country. She will be happy to answer your questions in Dutch, French and English.

And finally we also offer accommodation in Ouarzazate. Up to 6-8 people can stay in a beautiful apartment near Taourirt Kasbah. For more details we invite you to visit www.airbnb.be.
