CROSSING THE ROSE VALLEY & THE MGOUN CANYON

Overview

Difficulty
Duration
8 days
Best season
May to September
Accomodation
Hotel, Auberge, gîte, tents
Price
On demand

A 6-day trek through one of the most beautiful valleys of the ‘Grand Sud’, the Rose Valley (or Mgoun Valley), nested at the foot of the High Atlas. The name refers to the colour of the mineral of which the mountains are made.

Day 1: Ouarzazate - Boutaghrar (1704m)

Arrival at the airport and transfer by minibus or 4WD via El kalâa mgouna (route of 1000 Kasbahs) to Boutaghrar, the starting point of our hike where we will meet the muleteers. Overnight stay in an auberge.

Day 2: Boutaghrar – Agouti – Almdoun (1755m)

After breakfast we’ll start our trek in the direction of the village of Agouti where we’ll continue our walk and enter the gorges of agouti (3km of gorges) to reach the village of Alemdoun, Almdoun, a very beautiful, vast plain with a few small hamlets. Overnight stay in tents.

Day 3: Almdoun – Tighremt n’Tichki (2377m)

Today we will gradually walk up from the plain and cross several Berber villages. Our next bivouac in Tighremt n’Tichki is situated on 2377 meters.

Day 4: Tighremt n’Tichki - Tighremt n’Aït Ahmed (2235m)

A steep path will lead us to the Aït Ahmed Pass (2990m) from where we have a fantastic view on both sides of the mountains. We’ll descend on the other side to the confluence of 2 valleys: Ouililimt Valley and Mgoun Valley. Overnight stay in tents near the ruins of an old kasbah.

Day 5: Tighremt n’Aït Ahmed – Imi Nirkt (2046m)

Beautiful day of walking in the Mgoun Valley through many villages, campsite in Imi Nirkt at the entrance of the Mgoun Canyon.

Day 6: Imi Nirkt – Agerzaka (1736m)

Walking whole day in the river through one of the most spectacular areas of southern Morocco, the Mgoun Canyon. Overnight stay in tents or in an auberge.

Day 7: Agerzaka – Boutaghrar (1704m)

On our last day of hiking we will enter the Rose Valley again, we will cross some pretty, authentic villages (Issoumar,…) to finish our trip in Boutaghrar. Transfer to Ouarzazate, overnight stay in a hotel

Day 8: Ouarzazate – return flight

Got to catch that airplane!
